# Maintainer comment template

The 3-sentence reply for recurring "what is this?" questions across channels
(HN, Reddit, Discourse, Mastodon). Adapt the last line to the thread.

---

RoamingEye is an open-source 3D Earth that streams decades of NASA's monthly
satellite composites (vegetation, temperature, snow, land cover, …) straight
from GIBS into the browser — no backend, no account — and lets you chart any
point or drawn region across the full record, with provenance-stamped CSV
export. Open data honestly tops out around 30 m/pixel (Harmonized
Landsat-Sentinel powers the high-res study patches; the global monthly layers
are 1–2 km), and the app states that ceiling rather than hiding it. We're
looking for contributors — the graphics side has meaty tile-streaming
follow-ons (skirts, polar handling, 3D terrain) and there are groomed
good-first-issues at
https://github.com/zkWizard/RoamingEye/issues — happy to answer anything here.
