# Marine Biology: SST coverage provenance

## Contribution

- Added `src/lib/marineCoverage.ts`, a pure contract for describing supplied
  MODIS/Aqua sea-surface-temperature coverage, footprint context, cited source,
  source image dimensions, and accessible user copy.
- The contract labels its record as an SST observation and explicitly excludes
  marine-biology, abundance, habitat, health, causal, risk, and forecast claims.
- Added focused tests for water, coastal/land-mixed, land, zero-coverage, and
  invalid-metadata paths.

## Limits and next slice

The helper preserves supplied spatial context rather than deriving it from SST
pixels. It is not a biological observation. A follow-up can present this
coverage contract in the accessible probe or place-insight workflow.
